# Modules Map

- Dashboard: priority panel, room map, alerts, today's schedule, and activity feed.
- Residents: resident profile, contacts, documents, medical profile, timeline, and room assignment.
- Medications: medicine catalog, resident medicines, schedules, dose administration, shelves, and stock alerts.
- Nutrition and Kitchen: diet plans, meal plans, distribution, inventory, stock transactions, and waste.
- Reports and Finance: operational reports, financial reports, PDF, Excel, revenue, expenses, and summaries.
- Rooms: floors, rooms, occupancy, availability, and movement history.
- Staff: staff records, roles, shifts, and permissions.
